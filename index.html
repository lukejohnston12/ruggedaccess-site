Accessible outdoor adventures

Opening Colorado’s wild places for all.

Rugged Access Project is a 501(c)(3) that works to give people with disabilities guided access to Colorado’s backcountry using adaptive gear and trained professionals.

100% of online gifts go directly to Rugged Access program costs.
2025 snapshot
Excursions include adaptive backcountry hikes, accessible fishing trips, guided shooting experiences, and more.
Mission & approach

Trips built for access, dignity, and joy outside.

Rugged Access exists so people with disabilities don’t have to watch the mountains from a distance. We pair adaptive equipment with guides who listen, and itineraries shaped by each participant.

01 • Access

Gear that can handle real terrain

Off-road wheelchairs, trail-ready rigs, and adaptive setups tested on Colorado dirt, rock, and snow.

Trails Lakes Snow days
02 • Support

Guides who understand different needs

Small teams of volunteers and guides trained in accessible trip planning, transfers, and on-trail communication.

03 • Community

Trips that feel like team wins

We plan routes where everyone shares the effort and the celebration — the goal is belonging, not just reaching a viewpoint.
